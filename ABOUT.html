Who is IO?

I O is a set of experiences.

Modern cybernetic implants allow individuals to connect with the virtual world easier than ever before. But I O enables individuals to connect with one another. Supporting all modern mk7 implants and above, anyone can sign up and begin sharing experiences with users all over the world.

We provide a platform for travellers to share locations, for friends to share experiences, for strangers to learn about each other and themselves. Come and join the travellers.
